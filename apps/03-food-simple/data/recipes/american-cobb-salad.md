# American Cobb Salad

## Preparation Time
- Total Time: 30 minutes
- Prep Time: 30 minutes
- Cook Time: 0 minutes

## Meal Type
- Type: Lunch
- Temperature: Cold

## Ingredients
- For the salad base:
  - 1 head romaine lettuce, chopped
  - 1 head butter lettuce, chopped
  - 2 cups watercress, trimmed

- For the toppings:
  - 2 chicken breasts, grilled and diced
  - 6 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
  - 3 hard-boiled eggs, diced
  - 1 avocado, diced
  - 1 cup blue cheese, crumbled
  - 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  - 1/2 cup red onion, diced
  - 1/2 cup chives, chopped

- For the dressing:
  - 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  - 1/4 cup olive oil
  - 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  - 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  - 1 clove garlic, minced
  - Salt and pepper to taste

## Allergens
- Eggs
- Dairy (blue cheese)
- Pork (bacon)

## Preparation Steps
1. Prepare the salad base:
   - Wash and dry all lettuces
   - Chop romaine and butter lettuce into bite-sized pieces
   - Trim watercress
   - Combine in a large bowl

2. Prepare the toppings:
   - Grill chicken breasts, let cool, then dice
   - Cook bacon until crispy, then crumble
   - Hard-boil eggs, cool, peel, and dice
   - Dice avocado
   - Crumble blue cheese
   - Halve cherry tomatoes
   - Dice red onion
   - Chop chives

3. Make the dressing:
   - Whisk together vinegar, oil, mustard, Worcestershire sauce, and garlic
   - Season with salt and pepper

4. Assemble the salad:
   - Arrange lettuce on a large platter
   - Create rows of toppings across the salad
   - Garnish with chives
   - Serve with dressing on the side

## Required Tools
- Large bowl
- Large platter
- Knife and cutting board
- Measuring cups and spoons
- Whisk
- Small bowl for dressing
- Grill pan or skillet for chicken and bacon

## Description
A classic American salad that originated at the Brown Derby restaurant in Hollywood. The Cobb Salad is known for its distinctive presentation with rows of colorful ingredients arranged across a bed of mixed lettuces. The combination of grilled chicken, crispy bacon, hard-boiled eggs, creamy avocado, tangy blue cheese, and fresh vegetables creates a perfect balance of flavors and textures. This hearty salad is both visually appealing and satisfying, making it a popular lunch choice.
